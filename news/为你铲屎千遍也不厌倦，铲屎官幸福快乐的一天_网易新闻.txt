<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">2、早起散步</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">狗狗总是兴高采烈地散步，基本上是它遛我~不过有了狗狗之后，作息规律了好多，上班的时候也更有精神~</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">3、准备出门</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">不论洗漱还是化妆，做什么事情都要看着我，刚刚换好的衣服一蹭又都是狗毛，是不想我换衣服出门上班么？</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">4、离家上班</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">看着它的眼睛真舍不得离开它去上班~有时候说是因为路上堵车迟到，实际上都是因为不想离开狗狗….</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">5、开会</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">虽然身在公司，但是心里还是牵挂着独自在家的狗狗，不知道它自己在家过得怎么样？孤单么，还是又撕家了？</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">6、因工作外出时</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">被派到银行办理业务，排队的时候总是喜欢跟旁边人分享狗狗的照片~只要聊起宠物总是会有说不完的话题~</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">7、工作时间</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">每个养狗的人在上班的时候总会牵挂自己的狗狗，当然也包括我。特别是临近下班的时候，已经迫不及待想要回家见到它了~</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">8、下班回到家</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; line-height: 32px; white-space: normal;">这是一天中最开心的时刻~狗狗总是兴奋地摇着尾巴来门口迎接我，上班的疲惫顿时消散了~</strong>
<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important;">9、晚饭时间</strong>

<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box ! important; word-wrap: break-word ! important; line-height: 1.6;">10、晚安时间</strong>
